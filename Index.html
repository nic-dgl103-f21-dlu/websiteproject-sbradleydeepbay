About Us

Bradley Travel and Tours is a small boutique travel agency in Shawnigan Lake, B.C., owned and operated by Tara Bradley, a travel agent with 25 years of experience. Tara can credit her longevity in the industry to one simple thing: she loves making travel dreams come true. “I have climbed the Eiffel Tower, floated in a gondola in Venice, snuggled a koala, and snorkelled the Great Barrier Reef. There are still so many places on my bucket list, and every time I help my clients make their own travel dreams a reality, I feel like I am travelling too! I love seeing their smiles, and hearing about their adventures. It’s important to me that my clients have the best possible experience, and I want to share my knowledge and experience.”
